# Blog Hero Image Prompt

## 기본 프롬프트

```text
A wide cinematic blog hero image for a scholarly AI research and industry technology briefing aimed at researchers.
Scene: a refined academic research desk and digital observatory, combining the atmosphere of a quiet historical knowledge archive with contemporary AI research infrastructure.
On the desk are abstract research papers, data visualizations without readable text, translucent neural network maps, semiconductor-like circuit patterns, and a subtle robotic claw-shaped research instrument gently organizing knowledge fragments.
In the background, a calm panoramic field of glowing nodes, timeline-like light trails, and layered technology landscapes suggests emerging AI trends, papers, labs, startups, and industry movement.
Professional, academic, understated, elegant, intellectually serious.
No people, no portraits, no logos, no readable text.
Use deep navy, warm parchment, muted graphite, soft gold accents, and cool cyan light.
Composition should feel like a premium blog hero image, not an infographic or card news design.
Wide 16:9 aspect ratio, high detail, realistic editorial illustration, cinematic lighting, refined depth of field, spacious upper area suitable for a blog header overlay, sophisticated and trustworthy.
```
